Good to Know

Before you arrive

Booking window

Weekend retreats can usually be booked 3 days out; longer retreats fill 2–3 weeks ahead.

What's included

All meals, program access, and simple accommodation. Bring loose clothing and a torch for evening walks.

No experience needed

Every retreat opens with an orientation, so newcomers and experienced sitters begin on the same footing.
